Overview

Mission
Power 30 is committed in bridging the gap between academia and industry, empowering students to turn their knowledge into practical,impactful solutions. We aim to foster a culture of innovation and hands-on learning,preparing the next generation of leaders to tackle real-world challenges.
Collaboration
Through strategic collaborations with industry leaders and innovators, Power 30 offers students the opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects that provide valuable real-world experience. Our partnerships ensure students are at the forefront of technological and business advancements.
Creativity and Teamwork
At Power 30, we believe that the most groundbreaking ideas come from collaboration and creativity. Our students work in dynamic teams, blending diverse perspectives and skillsets to develop innovative solutions that push boundaries and inspire new ways of thinking.
Future Innovators
Power 30 is not just about learning—it's about leading. By equipping students with the skills, experience, and confidence to lead complex projects, we are preparing the next generation of innovators and industry leaders who will shape the future.
Industry Impact
Our projects are designed to solve real industry problems. With each collaboration, Power 30 students contribute to the success of partner companies by creating innovative solutions that not only address today’s challenges but also set new standards for the future.
