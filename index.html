Welcome to POWER 30

We are a dynamic, student-led club focused on solving real-world challenges through cutting-edge projects. Collaborating with industry leaders, we tackle problem statements provided by companies, offering students the opportunity to apply their skills, learn from professionals, and build innovative solutions.

Empowering Students, Driving Innovation

At POWER 30, we bridge the gap between academia and industry by taking on projects that address real-world challenges. Companies provide us with problem statements, and our talented group of 30 students works together to develop innovative, practical solutions. Our club is designed to foster teamwork, creativity, and technical excellence, ensuring our members gain invaluable experience while contributing to impactful projects. Turning Challenges into Opportunities

POWER 30 collaborates with leading companies to transform their problem statements into actionable projects. Our workflow includes: Analyzing Company Problem Statements: Understanding the core issues presented by our partner companies. Developing Solutions: Using a variety of technologies, including software development, data analysis, and design thinking to solve problems. Collaboration and Mentorship: Our students work closely with industry experts, gaining insights and feedback at every step of the process. Delivering Results: We present fully-functional prototypes and solutions to our partners, ready for real-world application. From concept to completion, our students gain hands-on experience in project development, fostering both technical and professional growth.
